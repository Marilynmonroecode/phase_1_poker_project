WELCOME TO POKER: Texas Hold'em

About

Texas hold 'em is one of the most popular variants of poker. Players are dealt two private cards, and five community cards are dealt face-up on the table. Players use these to make the best possible five-card hand.

Turn

The turn is the fourth community card dealt after the flop. The dealer burns a card, then reveals the turn card. A new betting round follows.
